Description

Lasagne alla bolognese

Ingredients

  • lasagna pasta
  • tomato sauce
  • mozzarella
  • parmesan
  • ground beef>

Instructions

  1. Boil lasagna pasta
  2. Make tomato sauce
  3. Put pasta in baking dish
  4. Put tomato sauce on pasta
  5. Put mozzarella on top
  6. Put in oven
  7. Take out of oven
  8. Put parmesan on top
  9. Put in oven
  10. Take out of oven
  11. Serve

Notes

Make sure to put the pasta in the baking dish before the sauce so it doesn't get soggy.
