Steak

Ingredients

  • filet mignon
  • olive oil
  • garlic
  • rosemary
  • thyme
  • salt
  • pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Place the steak on a baking sheet.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Roast for 15 minutes.
  6. Remove from oven and let rest for 5 minutes.
  7. Serve.
